# Two important motivation

## Truly Own Your Data

This is the most important motivation of it.

What's the "Truly Own Your Data"?

- **Local-First**, Own your data in local, in spite of the cloud.
- **Privacy-First** ,Privacy protection, no one can see your data except for you.
- **No lock-in**, No vendor lock-in, open source data schema
